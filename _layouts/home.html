About Me

Hello! I am currently working as a Research Assistant at the Digital and Document Analysis Lab at the National Forensic Service in Korea. I am developing and researching a program that uses computer vision and machine learning techniques to analyze and classify data such as facial images and electronic signatures. I am also writing papers related to my research. I am particularly interested in the data preprocessing phase for distinguishing genuine and forged images, and I am focusing on areas such as segmentation and feature extraction.

Furthermore, I hope that my research can contribute to the field of forensic science, aiding in criminal investigations and the judicial system, ultimately contributing to justice. My ultimate goal is to contribute to a better society through research for the public good.
